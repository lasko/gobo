First release published to FOSS app stores.

* Play live games against bots or open human challenges on OGS.
* Realtime board, captures, pass/resign, and stone-removal scoring.
* Watch live games and GoTV streams; spectate by link.
* Puzzles: browse tsumego collections (sortable) and solve with hints.
* Finished-game review, optional in-game chat, and Light/Dark/System themes.
* One permission (INTERNET), no analytics, no trackers.
